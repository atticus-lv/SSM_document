四种的对齐地面（激活项）方式，可以作用于网格与非网格物体

> Images are stored until you choose to remove them.

To share an image:

1. Open a message with someone
2. Select the **Send Media** button
3. Pick an image

![](//placehold.it/1400x900)

Supported types:

* JPEG
* PNG
* BMP
* GIF

Unsupported types:

* SVG
* WEBP
